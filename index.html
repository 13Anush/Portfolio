Customer segmentation using K-means clustering is a powerful strategy to optimize business decisions and drive growth. By identifying the optimal number of customer segments using the elbow method, businesses can visualize clusters to understand distinct customer groups effectively. This analysis, performed with Python libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, and Scikit-learn, is enhanced by interactive visualizations created with Matplotlib and Seaborn. Implementing K-means clustering categorizes customers into meaningful segments, enabling businesses to personalize marketing strategies and enhance customer satisfaction by addressing specific preferences. Ultimately, this approach helps maximize profitability and ROI by aligning strategies with actionable customer insights. I developed a Power BI dashboard for call center data analysis in collaboration with The Forage and PwC. This tool offers concise insights into key metrics and trends through interactive visualizations, including dynamic charts and tables. With robust filtering and drill-down features, it enables tailored analysis by timeframes, customer segments, and service channels. The dashboard supports trend identification, operational optimization, and strategy development, while interactive features like annotations and sharing options enhance collaboration and decision-making.
